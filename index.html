The Problem

Your eyes work harder
than you think.

On screens, people blink up to 66% less than normal. After hours of focus, the strain quietly adds up.

👁️

Reduced Blinking

Screen focus drops your blink rate dramatically, leaving eyes dry and irritated.

🔵

Blue Light Exposure

Prolonged blue light contributes to eye fatigue and disrupted sleep cycles.

🎯

Sustained Focus

Your eye muscles lock into a single focal length for hours, causing cumulative strain.

🧠

Mental Fatigue

Eye strain translates directly to headaches, brain fog, and reduced productivity.

The Solution

The 20-20-20 Rule

Recommended by ophthalmologists worldwide. Simple, effective, and backed by science.

20

Every 20 minutes

Take a short break from your screen during focused work sessions.

20

Look 20 feet away

Shift your gaze to a distant object — a wall, a tree, out the window.

20

For 20 seconds

Allow your eye muscles to fully relax and reset from sustained close focus.
